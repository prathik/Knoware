About

Get better ideas, review your thoughts and keep track of what is important. One of the most important things about life and progress is that you remember what is right and what is wrong.

KnoWare lets you efficiently track your thoughts. One thing you can notice is that there is no deleting ideas you can either approve them after review or reject them, the simple reason for this is once you get a thought in your mind, you cannot undo it and similarly once you key in your thoughts here, you can either approve them or reject them. This makes sure that you never forget them.

In this tool, you can never directly approve a thought, you have to wait for the required review period that is decided by you and then approve or reject it after the said period. Currently when you add the thought it is added in the review state but you can approve or reject it anytime, but this system is going to be changed soon.

This tool also reminds you about the pending review after the review time is over and the reminder can be configured to come over email and by the next year feature to send SMS to your phone, remind you on Facebook will also come.

How to use KnoWare?

  • Add an idea by going to Add Idea! page.
  • Set the date on which it was posted on and review time.
  • On the end of the review time you will be sent an email asking for reviewing the idea.
  • You can the assign a status as approved or rejected for an idea.
  • Approved ideas can be used and tracked.
  • Rejected ideas can be tracked and ensured that it is not used.
  • Rejected ideas can be sent back to review state.

Why use KnoWare?

  • Track cool ideas and don't forget them.
  • Form concepts and philosophies of life.
  • Many decisions of life are made based on some reasoning, track them, you can reuse the reasoning if they work or keep track of why the reasoning didn't work.
  • Put anything to test from scientific concepts to everyday small decisions.

Example of using KnoWare

  1. If you have an exam, you will make a study plan. Put that study plan on KnoWare and put the review time for 1 Week, check if you are meeting your goals with that study plan. If you are meeting your goals then approve the study plan and if you are not then reject it.
  2. You are a scientist and you need to check if your idea is good. Put the idea on KnoWare and set a review period. At the end of it you can approve or reject it. Later your collegue gets the same idea but sees your KnoWare result and his time will be saved. Note: Here we are using a group based KnoWare account.
